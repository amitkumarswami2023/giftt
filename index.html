ABOUT GIFTT

At GIFTT, we believe that true success comes from more than just technical or business knowledge. We are committed to developing human intelligence by equipping individuals with essential life skills, enabling them to excel in every aspect of life.

Through scientifically designed methodologies, we prepare students and professionals for real-world challenges. Our focus on Individual Transformation (IT), along with frameworks like ACE (Assessment Coaching Exposure) and FLP (Future Leadership Programs), ensures continuous growth at every stage of the journey.
