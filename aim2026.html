About
the Event

Bridging Innovation, Education, and Opportunity.

AIM (Academia Industry Meet) is a flagship initiative by GIFTT - Global Institute for Talent Transformation, bringing together academic leaders and industry pioneers on single platform.

A transformative event designed to connect academic excellence with industry innovation, creating pathways for collaboration, research, and workforce development that benefit students, institutions, and companies alike.

Together for Tomorrow — Bridging Minds, Building Futures.

Why This Event Matters
