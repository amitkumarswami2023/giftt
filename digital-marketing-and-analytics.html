FORMAT

Immersive learning experience

Live online sessions, AI labs, workshops & capstone project

DURATION

4 Months

16 sessions including AI tool labs, case studies & final capstone

ELIGIBILITY

Any Graduate / Working Professional

Designed for graduates, marketers, entrepreneurs & professionals with 0–8 years of experience

MODE

Live Online + Recorded Learning

Flexible executive-friendly format with weekend live sessions

Master AI-driven growth, performance marketing, content systems, automation & digital strategy to become a future-ready marketing leader

Programme Overview

01

Digital marketing is being fundamentally transformed by AI, automation, predictive analytics, and changing consumer behaviour.

02

Traditional marketers focused on campaigns, channels, and execution. Future-ready leaders must now design scalable growth systems, leverage AI for decision-making, and lead digital transformation initiatives.

03

This programme equips professionals to transition from tactical digital execution to strategic AI-powered marketing leadership.

Key Learning Outcomes

By the end of this programme, participants will be able to:

Build AI-powered digital growth systems

Design scalable content and automation workflows

Run AI-enhanced performance marketing campaigns

Build customer journeys powered by AI and personalization

Leverage predictive analytics for marketing decisions

Develop AI-first branding and differentiation strategies

Lead marketing teams and digital transformation initiatives
