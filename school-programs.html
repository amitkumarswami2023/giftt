Innovation Program

The EI Edge

The EI Edge: Master Your Emotions. Lead Your Life” is an experiential Emotional Intelligence (EI) program designed for students from Grades 4–12. It develops critical skills—self-awareness, emotion management, empathy, social skills, and responsible decision‑making—through age-appropriate, interactive modules. The goal: help students thrive academically and personally by transforming emotional literacy into lifelong capability.

Duration

8 sessions (over four weeks).

Skill Development Program

Talent Discovery Program (TDP)

The Talent Discovery Program (TDP) is your gateway to discovering your unique talents, enhancing your professional skills, and preparing yourself for success in the corporate world. This program is scientifically designed, using an assessment methodology that has been taken by over 20 million people globally, and over 20 thousand in India.

Duration

2 Weeks (Weekend Sessions) of intense learning, practical insights, and career-focused counselling.
