AI-Powered Leadership Programme for Tech Professionals

  • Home

  • AI-Powered Leadership Programme for Tech Professionals

FORMAT

Executive Learning Experience

Live online sessions, leadership simulations, AI labs & capstone project

DURATION

4 Months

16 sessions including leadership labs, AI strategy workshops & capstone

ELIGIBILITY

Graduates & Working Professionals

Designed for technology professionals with 3–15 years of experience

DELIVERY MODE

Live Online + Recorded Learning

Weekend-friendly format for working professionals

TIME INVESTMENT

4–6 Hours Weekly

Flexible weekly learning structure designed for busy professionals

Build leadership presence, strategic thinking, stakeholder influence & AI-enabled decision-making capabilities for future enterprise leadership

Programme Overview

01

Technology professionals often excel in execution but struggle to transition into enterprise leadership roles.

02

Technical expertise alone is no longer sufficient for career acceleration into management, strategy, or executive positions.

03

This programme enables participants to build leadership presence, strategic business acumen, AI fluency, stakeholder influence, and decision-making capabilities required to lead teams, functions, and transformation initiatives.

Key Learning Outcomes

By the end of the programme, participants will be able to:

Transition from technical expert to business leader

Build executive presence and strategic authority

Leverage AI to improve productivity, decision-making & innovation

Influence stakeholders and senior leadership teams

Understand business strategy, financial decision-making & governance

Scale teams, systems, and organizational impact

Prepare for Director, VP & CXO transitions

Who Should Attend
