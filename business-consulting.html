Programme Overview

01

Business consulting is being transformed by AI, faster decision cycles, and increasingly complex business environments.

02

Traditional consulting relied heavily on research, frameworks, and analysis. Today’s consultants must combine structured problem-solving, business acumen, client influence, financial literacy, and AI-enabled execution.

03

This programme prepares participants to think, communicate, and deliver like high-performing consultants while leveraging AI as a force multiplier.

Key Learning Outcomes

By the end of this programme, participants will be able to:

Think like a structured, hypothesis-driven consultant

Apply core consulting frameworks with judgment

Translate data into actionable business insights

Build high-impact consulting decks & proposals

Diagnose client problems and manage stakeholder expectations

Use AI tools to accelerate consulting workflows

Build consulting capabilities across strategy, operations, finance & digital transformation

Launch or accelerate a consulting career confidently
