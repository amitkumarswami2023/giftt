Project–Product–Process (P³) Excellence Programme

  • Home
  • /
  • Project–Product–Process (P³) Excellence Programme

FORMAT

Executive Weekend Learning Experience

Industry-led live sessions, workshops, simulations & capstone project

DURATION

3 Months | 72 Hours

8 intensive modules delivered over weekends

ELIGIBILITY

Graduates & Working Professionals

Designed for professionals with 0–8 years of experience across project, operations, product & business roles

DELIVERY MODE

Live Online

Weekend learning format with practical assignments and mentor feedback

TIME INVESTMENT

6 Hours Every Saturday & Sunday

Structured weekend schedule designed for working professionals

Build future-ready project leadership skills with strategic execution, product thinking, process excellence & AI-enabled decision-making

Programme Overview

01

Traditional project management training focuses heavily on frameworks and certifications.

02

Modern organizations require professionals who can integrate project execution, product thinking, process excellence, stakeholder leadership, and AI-enabled decision-making.

03

This programme goes beyond conventional PM training by combining strategic project delivery with hands-on application and real-world business execution.

Key Learning Outcomes

By the end of the programme, participants will be able to:

Lead projects with strategic clarity and execution excellence

Integrate product thinking into delivery decisions

Design and optimize scalable business processes

Apply Agile, Waterfall & Hybrid project models

Manage stakeholders, risks & governance frameworks

Use data and AI tools for smarter project decisions

Deliver end-to-end business solutions through live capstone execution

Who Should Attend

Designed for professionals across project, product, operations & business functions

Project Managers

Product Managers

Business Analysts

Operations Professionals

Program Managers

Consultants

Early Career Managers

Entrepreneurs

Curriculum Structure

MODULE 1: Strategic Foundations of Project Management

Setting context, mindset & execution strategy

Topics Covered
  • Modern project management fundamentals
  • Scope, time & cost balancing
  • Project lifecycle management
  • Agile vs Waterfall vs Hybrid models
Hands-on Learning
  • Project framework selection workshop
  • Execution strategy design
Outcome

Develop strategic project planning capabilities.

MODULE 2: Product Management – Discovery to Roadmap

Building the right solution before building it right

Topics Covered
  • Product thinking fundamentals
  • User discovery & validation
  • Prioritization frameworks
  • Product roadmap creation
Hands-on Learning
  • Product discovery workshop
  • PRD creation lab
Deliverable
  • Product roadmap
  • One-page product brief
Outcome

Build product-led execution capabilities.

MODULE 3: Process Management – Design, Optimize & Automate

Creating scalable operational systems

Topics Covered
  • Process mapping
  • Workflow optimization
  • Lean principles
  • Six Sigma basics
  • AI in process automation
Hands-on Learning
  • Process redesign workshop
  • Efficiency improvement sprint
Deliverable
  • Current-state process map
  • Future-state process design
Outcome

Design efficient and scalable processes.

MODULE 4: Agile & Hybrid Delivery in Practice

Speed with governance and control

Topics Covered
  • Agile delivery systems
  • Sprint planning
  • Release management
  • Hybrid project governance
Hands-on Learning
  • Sprint simulation
  • Delivery model design
Deliverable
  • Sprint plan
  • Hybrid execution framework
Outcome

Execute projects with flexibility and structure.

MODULE 5: Stakeholder Management, Communication & Leadership

Driving alignment and influence

Topics Covered
  • Stakeholder management
  • Executive communication
  • Leadership without authority
  • Negotiation frameworks
Hands-on Learning
  • Role plays
  • Communication simulations
Deliverable
  • Stakeholder communication plan
  • Executive project update
Outcome

Lead cross-functional teams effectively.

MODULE 6: Risk, Governance & Compliance

Structured decision-making and control

Topics Covered
  • Risk identification & mitigation
  • Governance models
  • Compliance frameworks
  • Escalation systems
Hands-on Learning
  • Risk simulations
  • Governance review scenarios
Deliverable
  • Risk register
  • Governance framework
Outcome

Build robust project governance systems.

MODULE 7: Data-Driven Project Management & AI Tools

Using analytics and AI for smarter execution

Topics Covered
  • Project metrics & dashboards
  • Forecasting models
  • Reporting automation
  • AI tools for PM workflows
Tools Covered
  • Microsoft Project
  • Jira
  • Notion AI
  • ChatGPT
Deliverable
  • Live project dashboard
  • Data story presentation
Outcome

Make data-backed project decisions.

MODULE 8: Capstone – Real-World Business Challenge

Applying integrated project, product & process excellence

Participants work in teams to solve a real organizational challenge.

Capstone Includes
  • Problem diagnosis
  • Product strategy
  • Process redesign
  • Execution roadmap
  • Final leadership presentation
Final Deliverable

Integrated business solution presented to expert panel.
