Elevate through Emotions

Global Emotional Agility Network (GEAN)

A closed, research-backed learning community for students and professionals to build self-awareness, emotional regulation, and conscious leadership together.

A space for meaningful dialogue, shared learning, and deeper reflection on human emotions. Because emotional intelligence was never meant to be explored alone.

Research-backed. Practice-driven. People-first.

Why This Community?

It helps students develop the human skills that matter beyond academics. Members gain insights from psychologists, educators, and industry leaders while building self-awareness, communication, resilience, and leadership abilities.

The community provides a safe space for meaningful discussions, mentorship, and real-world learning—helping students become emotionally intelligent, confident, and future-ready professionals.

What Makes This Community Different?

Grounded in Global Research

Built on neuroscience of emotions, Emotional Intelligence frameworks, and behavioural science.

Practical Tools, Not Just Theory

Simple practices you can apply in daily leadership, classrooms, and relationships.

Peer Learning & Reflection

Learn with leaders, educators, and professionals through guided reflection circles.

Closed & Safe Space

Honest conversations without judgement or noise.

Global Perspectives

Learn from diverse experiences across sectors and regions.

A community space where learning, reflection, and leadership conversations come to life.

Meet our Brand Ambassadors

Prof. (Dr.) Abha Singh

Prof. (Dr.) Abha Singh is a distinguished Senior Behavioural Psychologist, educator, and Professor of Practice in Psychology & Behavioural Sciences with nearly three decades of academic and applied expertise. A gold medallist for her doctoral research, she has authored 65+ national and international publications and multiple books across fields including Organisational, Positive, Cyber, and Spiritual Psychology. She has mentored numerous doctoral scholars and contributed significantly to behavioural research and education. Her work has been globally recognised with honours such as the Ambassador for Peace Award (Seoul), Peace Maker Award, Teacher’s Day Honour by the Ministry of AYUSH, and the Women Achiever Award.

Dr. Smita Singh (Dabholkar)

Dr. Smita Singh (Dabholkar) is an Associate Professor of Organizational Behaviour at the Institute of Management Technology (IMT), Nagpur, with over two decades of experience in academic and consulting. She holds a Ph.D. in Psychology from Nagpur University and specializes in organisational development, behavioural science, and HR management. An author and speaker, she actively shares insights on leadership, career readiness, and personal growth. She has also co-authored a book on Accelerated Action Learning with internationally recognised scholar Professor William J. Rothwell.
