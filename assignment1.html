What is E-Invoicing?

E-invoicing is the electronic generation, exchange, and validation of invoices in a structured digital format between businesses and tax authorities.

The UAE has introduced mandatory E-Invoicing with phased deadlines.

Implementation Phases

Phase A

Large Businesses

Revenue ≥ AED 50M

Deadline: 31 July 2026

Go Live: 1 Jan 2027

Phase B

SMEs

Revenue < AED 50M

Deadline: 31 March 2027

Go Live: 1 July 2027

Important Notes

  • B2C transactions are excluded
  • Government timeline: Oct 2027
  • Voluntary adoption starts July 2026
